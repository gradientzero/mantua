An evaluation harness is the precondition for everything else in the SLM playbook: you
cannot distill, fine-tune, or swap models safely without one. See
[[model-distillation]] for where this bites first.

Sketch of requirements (to be developed):

- Task-specific golden sets, versioned in git next to the model configs.
- Cheap enough to run on every training iteration.
- Direct comparison against the frontier-model baseline on the same set.
